// SCENARIO
A contract genomics lab receives whole-exome sequencing data for 6 patients across 2 oncology studies. Raw variant calls arrive as structured rows (simulating VCF→DB ingest). The ETL pipeline must: extract raw calls → apply QC filters (depth ≥20×, QUAL ≥30, allele frequency thresholds) → join against gene annotation and ClinVar significance tables → aggregate per-sample variant burden → load a clean, audit-logged delivery mart ready for the client biostatistics team.
